Project: TipCalculator

TipCalculator ia an android application used to calculate tip and total money for each person.

	-A simple and clean interface
	-Very easy to use
	-Two inputs can give a very comprehensive information

	-It takes two ingputs: amount and size of people
	-By doing some operations, produces as output both the amount of tip and the total amount each person should pay for a 15%, 20%, and 25% tip


Insrtuction:

	-Enter the price amount after "Check amount:" and enter the number of people after the "Party size:"
	-Click "COMPUTE TIP"
	-Then tips and totals(per person) will show as the nearest integer
